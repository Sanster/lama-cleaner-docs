# LaMa: Resolution-robust Large Mask Inpainting with Fourier Convolutions

- github: https://github.com/saic-mdal/lama
- paper: https://arxiv.org/abs/2109.07161

Lama Cleaner was initialy developed to work with LaMa model, it's the first model I saw generalizes surprisingly well to high resolutions (~2k❗️)
and achieves the excellent performance even in challenging scenarios, e.g. completion of periodic structures.
